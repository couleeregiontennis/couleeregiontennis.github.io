League Registration Payment

We use Zeffy for online payments because they are the only platform for nonprofits that doesn't charge transaction fees. This ensures that 100% of your registration fee goes directly to LTTA to cover court costs and balls.

Note: During checkout, Zeffy defaults to an additional "voluntary contribution" to their platform. You can change this amount to $0 if you prefer to only pay the league fee.
