2026 RULES REMINDER
  • Lineup Transparency: If teams can't agree on who plays first, the Home team must complete their official lineup sheet first.
TEAM SCORING
  • Standard Match: 1 participation point is awarded for showing up on time, plus 1 point for each set won (including tie-breaker sets). A 2-0 win usually results in 3 total points.
  • Forfeits & Defaults: In the event of a no-show or default, the winning team is awarded all 3 points. The defaulting team receives 0 points.
  • Subs: A 'Last Minute' sub found from players at the court is treated the same as any other sub and counts toward the participation point (no penalty).
