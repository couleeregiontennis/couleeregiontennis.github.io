# La Crosse Team Tennis Association (LTTA) Summer Tennis League - 2024 Rules & Responsibilities

## League Coordinator
*   **Role:** The LTTA elects a League Coordinator at a yearly pre-season meeting.
*   **2024 Coordinator:** Sally Ruud (resigned 8/15/24).
*   **Oversight:** LTTA is overseen by the Coulee Region Tennis Association (CRTA).
*   **Play Site:** La Crosse Green Island Park (13 outdoor courts, 2300 7th St South).
*   **Schedule:** Tuesday and Wednesday evenings, late May through early August.
*   **Times:** Matches rotate between 5:30 PM and 7:00 PM.
*   **Vacancy:** If the Coordinator is incapacitated, Captains appoint a temporary replacement.

## League Co-Coordinator
*   Elected at the pre-season meeting.
*   Assists the Coordinator and assumes duties if needed. (Vacant in 2024).

## Team Captain Responsibilities
*   **Liaison:** Communicates team needs to the Coordinator.
*   **Fees:** Collects all player fees and submits them by the 2nd week of play.
*   **Lineups & Scores:**
    *   Checks lineups (including subs) before play.
    *   Reviews scoresheets after matches.
    *   Totals team points.
    *   Signs/initials the scoresheet when matches are done (or when leaving early).
*   **Meetings:** Ensures team representation at pre-season meetings.
*   **New Players:** Helps new members understand rules and format.
*   **Withdrawal:** Must declare intention to return the following summer to hold the team spot; otherwise, they wait for an opening.

## Team Member Responsibilities
*   **Arrival:** Arrive 10 minutes before match time.
*   **Substitutes:**
    *   Players must find their own sub using the league list or a player from the opposite night.
    *   **Playing Up:** A player may play one skill level up (e.g., a #3 subbing for a #2) without penalty.
    *   **Interchangeable Levels:** #4s and #5s are interchangeable. #1s and #2s are interchangeable (2024 exception).
    *   **Playing Up >1 Level:** No match penalty, but player ranking may be re-evaluated if they win.
    *   **Playing Down:** Playing down one level or more results in **4 points to that side** and **10 points to the opponent**.
    *   **New Subs:** Must be approved by the Coordinator (via text/email/phone) prior to the match to avoid a "non-sanctioned player" penalty.
*   **Rostering:**
    *   Players play on one night only unless there is a shortage.
    *   Dual-night players pay fees for both nights.
    *   Shared positions (e.g., spouses) pay one fee.
*   **Fees:** $25 per player, paid by the 2nd week. Covers balls/courts.
*   **Leave of Absence:** Players can protect their spot for one year by declaring intent to return; the replacement player is temporary.
*   **Etiquette:**
    *   Shirts and shoes required.
    *   No throwing racquets or smashing balls.
    *   Inappropriate language/behavior may result in warnings, point penalties, or suspension.
    *   "Have fun and make new friends."

## Match Play Rules
*   **Start Times:** 5:30 PM and 7:00 PM.
    *   **Warm-ups:** Limited to 10 minutes (including serves).
    *   **No "First Ball In"** (FBI).
*   **Forfeits:**
    *   Effective at 5:45 PM and 7:15 PM if players are not present (unless weather delay).
    *   Late arrival can still play, but score is recorded as 0 points for latecomer, 10 for opponent.
*   **Time Limits:**
    *   5:30 PM matches must vacate by 7:00 PM.
    *   **Unfinished Matches:**
        *   Can move to another site if all agree (use own balls).
        *   Otherwise, stop and record score.
        *   **Winner:** Leading team gets 10 points.
        *   **Loser:** Trailing team gets 8 points.
        *   **Tie:** If total games are tied, both get 8 points.
*   **Balls:** 5:30 PM matches leave balls for 7:00 PM matches. 7:00 PM winners keep or recycle them.
*   **Scoring Format:**
    *   **No-Ad Scoring:** 15, 30, 40, Game. (Deuce = 1 deciding point; receiver chooses side).
    *   **Sets:** Best 2 of 3 sets.
    *   **Tiebreaks:**
        *   Set Tiebreak (at 6-6): 7-point tiebreak (win by 2).
        *   3rd Set (Split Sets): 7-point tiebreak (win by 2).
*   **Heat Rule:**
    *   **Index > 95°F:** Players may agree to start sets at 2-2.
    *   **Index > 104°F:** League cancelled by 4:30 PM.
*   **Rain:** Sessions are independent. Rain at 5:30 PM doesn't automatically cancel 7:00 PM.

## Team Standings
*   Determined by **Winning Percentage**: (Points Earned / Total Points Available).
*   **Format 1 (Singles/Doubles):** 50 total points.
*   **Format 2 (Doubles Only):** 40 total points.
*   **Bonus Point (2024):** 1 bonus point awarded if a team plays all matches with rostered players (no subs).

## Team Scoring System (Per Match)
*   **10 pts:** Winner of match (or leader when halted).
*   **8 pts:** Non-winner of a split set (e.g., lost in 3rd set tiebreak).
*   **8 pts:** Both teams in a halted match with tied games.
*   **8 pts:** Non-winner of a halted match (trailing in games).
*   **6 pts:** Non-winner of a straight-set match (e.g., 0-2).
*   **6 pts:** Retiree in 3rd set (Opponent gets 10).
*   **4 pts:** Retiree in 2nd set (Opponent gets 10).
*   **4 pts:** Side using a **higher-rated sanctioned player** (playing down). Opponent gets 10.
*   **2 pts:** Retiree in 1st set (Opponent gets 10).
*   **1 pt:** Side using a **non-sanctioned player**. Opponent gets 10.
*   **0 pts:** Forfeit/No-show/Late. Opponent gets 10.

## Team Organization
*   **Roster:** 7 players. Levels: #1, #2, #3, #3, #3, #4, #5.
*   **Doubles Combinations:**
    *   #1 & #2
    *   Any two #3s
    *   #4 & #5
*   **Age:** 18+ or graduating high school senior turning 18 by September.

## Skill Descriptions (Self-Rated)
*   **#1:** Advanced. Good anticipation, net play, lobs/overheads. Force errors.
*   **#2:** Power and spin. Solid footwork. Good first serve. Developing second serve.
*   **#3:** Dependable strokes. Variety of shots. Can place serve.
*   **#4:** Lacks dependability/variety. Can direct shots but lacks depth. Serve consistent but weak.
*   **#5:** Novice. Moderate placement, slow pace. Inconsistent serve.
